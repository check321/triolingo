**目标（Goal）：**
- 与房屋经纪人沟通并完成租房流程（Communicate with a real estate agent and complete the apartment renting process.）

**挑战：**
1. 向房屋经济人描述你对房屋的要求，包括：位置、价格、面积、户型、装修、交通等。
   Describe your housing requirements to the agent, including: location, price, size, layout, decoration, transportation, etc.
2. 与房屋经纪人约定看房时间
   Schedule an apartment viewing with the agent.
3. 与房屋经纪人协商租金与付款方式
   Negotiate the rental price and payment method with the agent.
4. 与房屋经纪人完成合约签订
    Complete the contract signing with the agent.
